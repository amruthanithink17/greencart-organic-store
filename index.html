About Us

Our Story

GreenCart was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make organic, healthy food accessible to everyone. What started as a small farmers' market stall has grown into a trusted online destination for organic produce.

We work directly with local farmers who share our commitment to sustainable agriculture. Our products are always fresh, never treated with harmful pesticides, and delivered with care to your doorstep.

At GreenCart, we believe that eating healthy shouldn't be complicated or expensive. That's why we've eliminated the middleman to bring you farm-fresh produce at fair prices.
